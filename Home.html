Welcome To Pefa Githurai Cathedral

At Pefa Githurai, everyone is welcome, and we invite you to join us on this journey of faith.

All Nations Gospel Church (PEFA) stands as a beacon of hope and enlightenment,
where seekers of truth find solace in the comprehensive teaching of the Bible,
guiding them towards a profound relationship with Christ and a life filled with purpose.

Our Mission

  • To serve our community with love and compassion
  • To spread the teachings of Christ through outreach programs
  • To provide a welcoming and inclusive environment for all

Our Vision

  • To be a beacon of hope and inspiration for those in need
  • To foster spiritual growth and development in our congregation
