About Us

Welcome to the PEFA Githurai Cathedral, where we are committed to spreading the love and teachings of Jesus Christ to our community and beyond.

Founded in 1978, our church has been a beacon of hope, faith, and love for over 36 years. Our mission is to stand as a beacon of hope and enlightenment, where seekers of truth find solace in the comprehensive teaching of the Bible, guiding them towards a profound relationship with Christ and a life filled with purpose.

We believe in the power of worship, prayer, and fellowship to transform lives and build a stronger community. Our church offers a variety of ministries and programs to serve people of all ages and backgrounds.

At PEFA Githurai Cathedral, everyone is welcome, and we invite you to join us in our journey of faith and service.
